Surpassing existing Bayesian phylogenetic inference tools in speed and accessibility, Delphy allows researchers to immediately begin running and viewing their data with a simple FASTA file upload. Once sufficient samples have been generated, users can investigate their data by navigating the phylogeny of a summary tree, exploring and comparing mutations, and layering in their own metadata â€” all within Delphy. Data uploaded into Delphy is not sent to a remote server and information viewed within the tool will always remain secure, local, and fully within your control.

The Delphy tool has been developed by Fathom Information Design in close collaboration with Patrick Varilly, Ph.D., of the Sabeti Lab at the Broad Institute of Harvard and MIT.
