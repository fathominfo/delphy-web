What data do we collect? (top)

Delphy and the Site do not collect personal data or personally identifiable information. Delphy and the Site do collect:

  • Your anonymized IP address;
  • The date and time of the request;
  • The title and URL of the page being viewed;
  • URL of the page that was viewed prior to the current page (referrer URL);
  • The screen resolution being used;
  • The time in your time zone;
  • The files that were clicked and downloaded;
  • The links to an outside domain that were clicked;
  • The time it takes for webpages to be generated by the webserver and then downloaded by you (page generation time);
  • Your approximate location: country, region, city, approximate latitude and longitude;
  • Your browser’s main language (Accept-Language header) and user agent (User-Agent header); and
  • Your browser, operating system, type of device being used (desktop, tablet, mobile, etc.), brand, and model.

If you don’t want us to collect this information, you can opt-out.

What are your data protection rights? (top)

Fathom would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

  • The right to access — You have the right to request from us copies of your personal data. We may charge you a small fee for this service.
  • The right to rectification — You have the right to request that Fathom correct your personal data if you believe it is inaccurate. You also have the right to request Fathom to complete the data you believe is incomplete.
  • The right to erasure — You have the right to request that Fathom erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to restrict processing — You have the right to request that Fathom restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to object to processing — You have the right to object to Fathom’s processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to data portability — You have the right to request that Fathom transfer the personal data that we have collected on your to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us.

Cookies (top)

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information. When you visit our websites, we may collect information from you automatically through cookies or similar technology. For further information, visit allaboutcookies.org.

How do we use cookies? (top)

We only use first-party cookies. We use cookies to help us learn how well Delphy performs. We also use cookies to understand, improve, and research features, and services, including to create logs and record when users access our Site from different devices.

We provide the list below to give you transparency into the cookies used by Delphy and the Site. While we do our best to keep this table updated, please note that the number and names of cookies and other technologies may change from time to time.

Cookies Source Purpose Lifespan
_pk_id delphy.fathom.info For storing anonymized information, such as a unique visitor ID 13 months
_pk_ref delphy.fathom.info For storing attribution information, namely the referrer you initially used to visit the website 6 months
_pk_ses, _pk_cvar, _pk_hsr delphy.fathom.info For storing analytics-related data for the visit 30 minutes
_pk_testcookie delphy.fathom.info For checking whether your browser supports cookies A few seconds
matomo_sessid chronicle.fathom.info For storing your data collection preference and ensuring website security 30 minutes

How can you manage your cookies? (top)

You can set your browser not to accept cookies, and the above website tells you how to remove cookies from your browser. However, in a few cases, some of our website features may not function as a result.
